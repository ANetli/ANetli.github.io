Welcome back, Dear Student!

The Session Layer (Layer 5 of the OSI model) manages and controls the dialog (sessions) between two devices. It is responsible for: Establishing a session (start communication) Maintaining it (keep it active and synchronized) Terminating it (end communication gracefully) Example: In a video call, the session layer makes sure the call connection is established, kept alive, and closed properly.
